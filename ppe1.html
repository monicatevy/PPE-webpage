MediaTek86's Network

MediaTek86 is a network that gathers every managers of libraries from the department of Vienne (86), in France. As their network is growing, MediaTek86 called InfoTech Service 86 (ITS86) to develop their softwares.

ITS86 is an IT company that provides services such as : website and software development, web hosting, cloud solution and IT maintenance.

As a web developer junior, I'll be in charge of the software development. ITS86's team will be a great support throughout the project, from the app design to its release.

Software

MediaTek Management

MediaTek Management is a human resources software designed to make employee and leave management easy. The main features are : add/delete employees and modify their informations, for each employee; and you'll be able to add/delete/modify information about the leave.
